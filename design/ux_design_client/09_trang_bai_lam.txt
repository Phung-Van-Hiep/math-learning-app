                        CHI TIẾT CÁC THÀNH PHẦN
================================================================================

1. TABS
   - 3 tabs: Đang làm, Đã hoàn thành, Tất cả
   - Active tab: Blue background, bold
   - Inactive: Gray text
   - Badge count: Small circle với số
   - Transition: Smooth fade

2. BÀI ĐANG LÀM CARDS
   - Background: Light yellow (#FFFBEA)
   - Border-left: 4px orange
   - Progress bar: Visual indicator
   - Time tracking: Realtime if possible
   - Continue button: Primary blue
   - Delete button: Red outline

3. BÀI ĐÃ HOÀN THÀNH CARDS
   - Background: Light green (#E8F5E9) if passed
   - Background: Light red if failed
   - Border-left: 4px green/red
   - Score: Large, bold
   - Stars: Visual rating
   - Action buttons: View, Retry, Download

4. PROGRESS BAR
   - Height: 20px
   - Background: Light gray
   - Fill: Gradient blue
   - Border-radius: 10px
   - Percentage text: Inside bar

5. FILTERS
   - Dropdown: Width 150px
   - Border: 2px solid #E0E0E0
   - Focus: Blue border
   - Options: Type, date, score

6. BUTTONS
   - Continue: Blue (#2196F3)
   - View: Blue outline
   - Retry: Orange (#FF9800)
   - Delete: Red (#F44336)
   - Download: Green (#4CAF50)
   - Height: 40px
   - Border-radius: 20px
